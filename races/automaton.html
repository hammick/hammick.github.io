Automatons

Electricians and mechanics can create robots; infusing these with certain magics can create a living, feeling being out of these robots. Through their soul, they can feel emotions, thought, and awareness, but physical feelings, like pain or fatigue, or physical abilities depend on how they are built. Still, these living robots can be found now and then in the world, sometimes living among other populations. Because of their specific physical properties or uses, they are sought for their excellence at certain types of work. The feelings of robots are not entirely understood but seem to be identical to real living races, and they can be raised, affected, and conditioned all the same.
