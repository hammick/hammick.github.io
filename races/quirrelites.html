Quirrelites

An incredibly diverse race of bug people. They can take on many forms, resembling bugs with bug or hummin forms, or completely unique forms, sizing from hummin size to barely visible. Because of the vastness and diversity of this race, all sorts of personalities can be found among them. As a collective, though, they are a bit more primitive than the rest of the world. Still, they have a language, cities, tools, and societies, and plenty of mechanical systems, and they are beginning to use electricity.
