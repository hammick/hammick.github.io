Bloomiimabla (BLOO-mee-mah-bla)

An exotic race of fungal people. Found deep in the depths of mushroom forests (or sometimes normal forests), these people are usually seen only by lost adventurers. They are relatively primitive, living in villages with improvised structures, wielding melee weapons, and speaking a language distinct from all others. They take many unique forms, some humminid, some not. Though primitive, they are said to be a very old and wise race, and connected strongly to nature and the world.
