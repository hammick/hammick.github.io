Earthin

Though each one has a different tale as to their race's origin, Earthins are definitely an old race. Living in a vast underground world, these thick-skinned, round and chubby humminids have light skin and dark fur, and claws on their four limbs for digging. They also are long adapted to eating underground critters and minerals with their snout-like incisored mouths. Social creatures, cut off from the craziness of the outside world, they are compassionate and cooperative, though they are very unfamiliar with culture that is not their own, and seem very strange to outsiders. They also are less sophisticated and advanced, and definitely not as bright as other races, but they are incredibly hard and reliable workers, and excellent at digging, forging, and building. They can walk on two or four legs with ease, and have an excellent sense of direction. They seldom leave their underground homes, and one living on the surface is quite rare.
