Spriteling

One of the stranger races on the planet, Spritelings superficially resemble hummins but are typically much shorter and more childlike. They are extremely energetic and, despite their small stature, they are capable of incredible acrobatic feats; being able to jump several times their own body height is considered normal from their perspective. Their primary limiting factor is that they are generally quite frail. They have to make up for their weakness with agility in combat. Their disposition tends towards friendly and outgoing. Strangely, this personality tends to persist regardless of how they actually behave; even the most sadistic of them will seem to act with a childlike glee. It is almost impossible to predict the actions or intentions of an unknown Spriteling because of this. They tend to be highly adventurous, often venturing out into the world on a whim, so one can expect to find individuals almost anywhere in the world.

At the center of their civilization is a small order of elite warriors called the Temporals. These individuals specialize in using time magic to their advantage, and often carry enchantments that will rewind them if they die. Other beings rarely perceive this magic being used, and instead will see the warrior as being seemingly supernaturally lucky and skilled. Physical Description

They have similar traits to hummins, but they are smaller and more childlike.

Standard Racial Traits

  • Ability Score Racial Traits: Spritelings gain +2 Dex, -2 Con, and +2 Cha.
  • Type: Spritelings are humanoids.
  • Size: Spritelings are Small creatures and thus gain a +1 size bonus to their AC, a +1 size bonus on attack rolls, a -1 penalty to their CMB and CMD, and a +4 size bonus on Stealth checks.
  • Speed: Spritelings have a base speed of 30 feet.
  • Languages: Spritelings begin play speaking Common.

Defense Racial Traits

  • Energetic: Spritelings receive a +4 racial bonus on Constitution checks and Fortitude saves to avoid fatigue and exhaustion, as well as any other ill effects from running or forced marches.

Feat and Skill Racial Traits

  • Skill Bonus: Spritelings have +2 racial bonus on Acrobatics checks.
  • Skill Training: Acrobatics and Climb are always considered class skills for spritelings.

Movement Racial Traits

  • Fleet-Footed: Spritelings receive Run as a bonus feat and a +2 racial bonus on initiative checks.
  • Jumper: Spritelings are always considered to have a running start when making Acrobatics checks to jump.
