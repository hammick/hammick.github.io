Miphin

A race of fish-like humminids who live in the oceans and seas. Their scaled skin can be many vibrant colors, solid or multicolored, their bellies white. Miphin live in large colonies and can breathe in air and water. They are very sensual, artistic, and passionate people, yet they tend to live solitarily or in small groups, and most don't care for others of their kind or other races, ranging from welcoming to apathetic to unwelcoming to outsiders. Still, they are in-tune with their emotions and are innately curious, and always eager to contribute to society (theirs or others) nonetheless.

The Miphin have bodies adapted to underwater vocalizing. Their voices are high-pitched and project; nonetheless, they have mouths that can articulate the specific words of their language, and with practice they can pronounce words in Common.
